Welcome

I'm Michelle Ruby Johnson, and this is the design system for my portfolio website, which is currently in development. This design system will outline the typography, colors, and logos used on my portfolio, as well as several important components of the design, like the navigaion and buttons. I'll also define the layouts of a few key portfolio pages

The goal of this design is to be a minimalistic backdrop to my work, while also conveying some themes about my personality and my work. I sought to express an attitiude of warmth and friendliness. I hope users feel a bright, creative energy, while also maintaining an air of simplicity and pragmatism.
