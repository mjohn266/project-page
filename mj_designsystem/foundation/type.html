Typography

In this design, the typography is intended to convey a balance between creativity and simplicity, optimism and pragmatism. Dolly is a warm, friendly typeface with serifs that give it a more traditional feel. Runda is a very simple sans-serif font that lends an uncluttered modernism to the design.

Font Family: Dolly; serif and Font Family: Runda, sans-serif;
Scale: Perfect Fourth (1.33)
Base Size: 16 px
